My Background

My background and undergraduate degree is as an artist; I have a BFA in Illustration and Graphic Design from the Massachusetts College of Art. I've had a long and winding career from freelance illustration to web design and interaction design but never formal classes in web development. As a recent BC new hire and Woods College of Advancing Studies student in the Certificate Program in Information Technology, I was delighted to see that I can at last get formal training.

Finally, I don't know anything about CSS pre-processors, GitHub, or headless CMS. Hopefully, I can learn these things through this class.
