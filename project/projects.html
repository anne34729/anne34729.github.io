Experience

Drawing and painting is my greatest talent since I was a wee-one. Consequently I attended the Massachusetts College of Art, majoring in Illustration and Graphic Design thinking to make a profession out of it. I do not regreat this decision as I have enjoyed a wonderful career in digital design. In fact it turned quite lucrative and now I'm really loving front-end coding and development. I will soon complete a Certificate in Information Technology at Boston College.

But back to my innate fine-art skills. I sometimes wonder if I could have made it as a painter or freelance illustrator. How would life have been different? Could I have become quite great? And more generally, can anyone in this day and age with almost 8 billion people on the planet, distinguish themselves as a artist?
