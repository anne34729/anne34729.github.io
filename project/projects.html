Experience

Drawing and painting was my greatest joy since I was a wee-one. Consequently I was enrolled in many courses, the Museum School and art shows. Subsequently, I attended the Massachusetts College of Art, majoring in Illustration and Graphic Design thinking to make it my profession. This was wise as I embarked on a wonderful career in digital design. Digital design morphed quite naturally to coding, another great opportunity for creative invention. I've comtinued this study and will soon receive a Certificate in Information Technology from Boston College.

However sometime I sometimes muse about the alternative path, the life of a pure painter or fine artist How would life have been different? Could I have become great? More generally, can anyone on a planet of 8 billion people distinguish themselves as a artist?
